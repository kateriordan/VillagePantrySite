Village Pantry Convenience Store

Our Y101 project team created this site to help customers more easily purchase goods from the Village Pantry convenience store.

Our History:

The fiirst Village Pantry store was opened in 1966 in Muncie, Indiana. It was founded in Yorktown, Indiana as a divion of the Marsh super market company. There are now more than 400 locations throughout the Midwest, mainly in residential neighborhoods, tailoring products and services to local customers. In more than 100 of the locations, fresh sandwiches and donuts are made on-site each day.
